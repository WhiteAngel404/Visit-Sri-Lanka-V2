Booking Tips

Advice for finding the best accommodation

Book Early

December-March is peak season. Book 2-3 months in advance for best rates and availability.

Direct Booking

Many hotels offer better rates or perks when booking directly. Always compare prices!

Read Reviews

Check recent reviews on multiple platforms. Look for consistency in feedback.

Location Matters

Consider proximity to attractions. Staying central can save transport costs.
