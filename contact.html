Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent requests, please call us directly or use WhatsApp.

Can I customize a tour package?

Absolutely! We specialize in customized itineraries. Just let us know your interests, budget, and travel dates, and we'll create a personalized tour for you.

Do you require a deposit to book?

Yes, we typically require a 30% deposit to confirm your booking, with the balance due 30 days before your arrival. We'll provide detailed payment instructions upon confirmation.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before arrival receive a full refund minus processing fees. 15-29 days: 50% refund. Less than 14 days: no refund. Travel insurance is recommended.
